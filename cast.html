Main Characters

Alex Paws

Alex Paws is a dog who lives with his owner Pedro. An ordinary dog... Yes, he is an ordinary golden retriever. What do you expect? Umm... He likes bones and chocolate. Wait a minute! Dogs don't eat chocolate!

Pedro Perez

Pedro Perez is a college student who has a dog Alex Paws. He is the president of Pawesome club (formerly Pawesome and Math Club). He is respected by many for trying to find out Pawesome's secret identity.

Pangil

Pangil is a wolf from the forest north of where Alex and Pedro lives. He always seem mad or angry or at least nonchalant, even if he is not. He often hangs out with Ertle because, even though he lives in a forest, the forest has a very small population of animals, and even if there are animals, many fear him.

Ertle

Ertle is a box turtle that hangs out with Pangil. He is not dumb; he is just slow...

Pawesome

Pawesome is the superdog of Pangilan and beyond! Even I, the author, wonder where he came from...
