There are two main landmasses. The one that comprises the provinces of Lîroti, Lopulân, and horibama is called korolan. The one that comprises libodabo, lobolan, pangilan, inabalupa, damidō, asolan, tumbi valley, mikakkō, damshanlan, and mîkelan is called mampula.

—

Wolf Territories

Wolves are territorial. Most territories are within Dapangisangado, Libodabo, Lobolan, and Pangilan. Hunters (humans and other animals) in wolf territory are prohibited to hunt unless allowed by the alpha. (Note: "Alpha" is just a title.)
