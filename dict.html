If you cannot find an entry. Most probably it is a compound word or forms with endings. If possible, find the "noun form" of the word. For example, if you are looking for the word "inaugurate" and there is no output, try "inauguration"
Simple Grammar Forms
  • Nouns do not have prefixes and suffixes in the dictionary. Etymology does that for nouns. (Note that if a noun is a compound noun, best to enter the root word)
  • Verbs either have an .u or .ru ending.
  • Adjectives have an .ng, (g) or na
  • Adverbs have .ku ending
